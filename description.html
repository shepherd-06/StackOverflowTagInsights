Collapsible Tree of Tags and Questions

This visualization represents a hierarchical breakdown of programming collectives, tags, and questions.

Each collective (e.g., 'Mobile Development') is represented as a top-level block. Clicking on a collective expands it to show associated tags (e.g., 'android', 'ios'). Clicking on a tag reveals additional question statistics, including total questions, top and least voted question scores, and median statistics for votes and answer counts.

Interaction Guide:

  • Hover over a segment to see the label and value.
  • Click on a collective to expand it and reveal tags.
  • Click on a tag to see detailed question statistics in a bar chart below.
  • The chart is interactive and updates automatically as you explore different segments.
